Rules

  1. Red tiles are impassible, you cannot walk on them.
  2. Yellow tiles are electric, they will electrocute you.
  3. Orange tiles are orange scented, they will make you smell delicious.
  4. Blue tiles are water, swim through them if you like, but the piranhas hate the smell of oranges.
  5. If a blue tile is next to a yellow tile, the water will also zap you.
  6. Purple tiles are slathered in slippery lemon-scented soap, a scent which the piranhas do not like.
  7. Green tiles are chaos tiles, they will regenerate the puzzle.
  8. Pink tiles don't do anything. Step on them all you like.
